Our Story

At The School's Oven, we believe in bringing people together through the power of freshly baked goods. Our story began with a simple idea: to create a space where everyone can enjoy the comforting taste of home-baked treats. From our signature croissants to our warm pretzels, every item is made with love, care, and the finest ingredients.

Whether you're stopping by for your morning coffee or picking up a sweet treat for a special occasion, we are dedicated to making your experience unforgettable. Come visit us and taste the difference at The School's Oven.
